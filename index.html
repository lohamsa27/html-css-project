About Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs were a diverse group of reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic Era, over 230 million years ago. They ruled the Earth for more than 160 million years, evolving into various forms, sizes, and behaviors.

Types of Fossils

  • Body Fossils – Bones, teeth, and shells preserved over millions of years.
  • Trace Fossils – Footprints, burrows, and marks left by movement.
  • Mold & Cast Fossils – Impressions in sediment that later turned to stone.
